Services

IBS - Irritable Bowel Syndrome

A chronic gastrointestinal disorder causing abdominal pain, bloating, and altered bowel habits.

IBD - Inflammatory Bowel Disease

An umbrella term for chronic inflammation of the digestive tract, including Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis.

Crohn’s Disease

A type of IBD causing inflammation anywhere along the digestive tract, leading to pain, diarrhea, and weight loss.

GERD - Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease

A condition where stomach acid frequently flows back into the esophagus, causing heartburn and discomfort.

Gall Bladder Diseases

Disorders affecting the gallbladder, often involving gallstones or inflammation (cholecystitis)

Peptic Ulcer Disease

Open sores that develop on the inner lining of the stomach or upper part of the small intestine due to acid erosion.

Celiac Disease

An autoimmune disorder triggered by gluten ingestion, leading to small intestine damage and nutrient malabsorption.

H. Pylori Infections

Bacterial infection that affects the stomach lining and is a major cause of ulcers and gastritis.
